Consultant-led cancer screening and early detection in London

Book an Initial Consultation

OncoChecks provides specialist cancer screening designed and led by a senior consultant medical oncologist. We help you understand your personal cancer risk, navigate appropriate diagnostic tests, and receive clear, expert interpretation of results.

Screening and imaging are arranged through trusted partner providers in London, with every decision guided by consultant oversight rather than automated testing or generic packages.

The lifetime risk of cancer for an individual is approximately 1 in 2. This service is designed for people who want to be proactive about their health, understand their risk, and reduce the chance of a late or advanced cancer diagnosis.

From your first appointment, you receive expert-led advice, personalised screening recommendations, and a clear plan for next steps.
