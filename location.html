By Rail:

The nearest train stations are Liverpool Lime Street and Central Station.
The House is located 15 minutes walk from Liverpool Lime Street and 20 minutes walk from Central Station diagonally opposite Liverpool Cathedral.

By Bus:

From Lime Street Station catch the Number 82 Bus and get off on Berry Street. The House is then a short walk from there located diagonally opposite Liverpool Cathedral. From Queen Square catch the Number 4 bus and get off at Hope Street. Follow signs to the Cathedral; The House is located diagonally opposite.the Cathedral.

By Car:

From M6 North or South exit at junction 21A and then take the M62 (West for Liverpool). At the end of the M62 follow signs for City Centre and Cathedral. From Tunnels (Cheshire) follow signs for City Centre and also Cathedrals The House is located diagonally opposite Liverpool Cathedral.
