How It Works

Step1

Initial Consultant Consultation

A detailed review of your risks, family history, and concerns.

Step2

Personalised Screening Plan

Your consultant recommends the most appropriate tests or imaging.

Step3

Screening and Diagnostics

Blood tests or imaging arranged with trusted London partners.

Step4

Results and Follow-up

Your consultant explains everything clearly and discusses any next steps.

Step5

Prevention and Ongoing Health Plan

Your consultant creates a personalised prevention plan that may include:

Diet and nutrition guidance based on clinical evidence

Lifestyle adjustments to reduce cancer risk

Monitoring intervals tailored to your risk profile

Advice on sleep, stress, and physical activity

Supplement or medication recommendations only where evidence supports them

A long-term roadmap for future screening
